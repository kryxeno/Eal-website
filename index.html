EAL S3 Teams

The EAL is currently in its third season. An ever increasing competitive level pushes the players to their limits. Some new contenders have taken the EAL by storm, securing their position as a force to be reckoned with.

  • Get Colonized

    Domination comes easy to them. They are the team to beat in this competition.

  • 99 Woodcutting

    One of the original teams. They won in season 1 and got 2nd place in season 2.

  • Acceleration

    Another original team. This team has rotated players a few times, but now has a solid roster that can challenge for the win.

  • Tax Evasion Gaming

    TEG has had its ups and downs, but during Season 3 they established their fighting power by securing the 4th spot.

  • Rising Tides

    A new team, comprised of various free agents. This teams of individually talented players has yet to show they are a top contender.

  • Exile Gaming

    Exile is another new team. They have been rotating around 6th and 7th place with hit or miss performances, but is going in to playoffs.

  • Dirty Dancers

    DiD is a ragtag group of friends, giving organised teamplay a try. They have had a flurry of bad luck in season 3, but their team has potential.

  • Lockin

    Lockin is a relatively low ranked team. This combined with some issues has meant that they havent been able to show in Season 3.
