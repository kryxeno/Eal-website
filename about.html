About EAL

Lead by Tim "Bats" van Ingen

The European Amateur League (EAL) is the name of the amateur league of legends esports championship, lead by Tim van Ingen. This league accepts players up to Diamond 1 and is mainly focused on developing the talents of lower ranked players through organized teamplay.

There are currently 8 teams competing in the third season. The season will exist out of 7 weeks of round-robin tournament play, followed by a knock-out stage comprised of the top 6 teams.

Learn more

  • Where it started

    It all started with a group of people who had nothing to do during the quarantine. To keep things fun, they started a weekly competition to see who would take the throne. This has since flourished into a full competition, with a production team behind it.

  • Season 1

    The league in season 1 existed out of 6 teams, all created through friends. Teams included: Team Turbo (now called 99 Woodcutting), Tax Evasion Gaming, One Mic Gang, Gentlemen, AHQ Flash Assasins and De Zaanse Prutsers. Team Turbo (99W) managed to win the season 1 championship.

  • Season 2

    The competition started to become a lot more serious in season 2. There were a few additions in season 2: Get Colonized, Acceleration, Time To Throw, Last Place Legionaires and Free Agent Community. Get Colonized convincingly won EAL season 2.

  • Why join EAL?

    If you are done with grinding solo queue, or can't keep up with all the master players in other competitions, this is the place for you. At EAL you can grow in a friendly competitive environment, and fight for glory. You can join as a team or as a free agent.

  • Special events

    In between seasons we will often organize some special events. This could come in the form of a 1v1's tournament, valorant tournament, minecraft server, you name it. This way even people who don't play league can interact with and play in our community.

  • Future plans

    EAL Esports has a lot of future plans. This includes but is not limited to: academy league, podcasts, one-off tournaments and many more. Join the discord to see when these features will be added!
