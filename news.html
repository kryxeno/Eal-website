On to playoffs!

20-SEP-21

The regular season has just ended, and the standings are in. The playoffs format has been determined.

Starting with match 1 in the quarterfinals, Exile Gaming will fight against Acceleration in a best of three. The other quarterfinal is match 2. Here Tax Evasion Gaming will take the fight to Rising Tides. Further matches will be determined later.

Exile Gaming makes it!

24-SEP-21

Despite having to forfeit both of their games, Exile Gaming manages to take the 6th spot in playoffs!

They have had a rocky season. Having had to forfeit 4 games this season, the likely hood of them getting into playoffs was quite low. Dirty Dancers needed to win one match in the last week, but they weren't able to get one and they barely missed out.

GET Disbanding?

20-SEP-21

Rumours are starting to spread. Is Get Colonized going to split in Season 4, or is all of this just fake news?

An anonymous source said: "It seems more and more likely that GET will break into two parts next season". What does this mean for the upcoming season? Will the other teams finally get a chance at that sweet champion spot? We can only wait and see.
