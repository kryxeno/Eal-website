How to join EAL

Signups are currently open.

EAL season 4 starts in 1 month.

As a TEAM

So you have gotten together a team and you want to play in the upcoming season? This is what you do.

  1. 1. Join the Discord.

  2. 2. Read the rules very thoroughly.

  3. 3. Fill in this form.

  4. 4. Join the battlefy with your team.

  5. 5. Get ready for the start of the season!

As a PLAYER

So you want to join, but don't have a (full) team yet? This is what you do.

  1. 1. Join the Discord.

  2. 2. Read the rules very thoroughly.

  3. 3. Give yourself the free agent role and advertise yourself in the player transfers channel.

  4. 4. Once you have gotten together a team, follow the steps in "As a TEAM".
